The CLA Instructional Model

We bridge the gap between high school graduation and professional employment. Students complete core requirements online while earning Technical College Certificates (TCC) through our partners.

Academic Core

Fully online, self-paced, and mastery-based courses including Real-World English, Algebra, and Physical Science.

Technical Training

Hands-on skills training at approved technical college partners. (Note: Transportation is student-provided).
