The CLA Instructional Model

CLA operates under an Accelerated Career Diploma model, prioritizing workforce readiness and technical credentials. Students complete core academic requirements online while pursuing Technical College Certificate (TCC) programs.

Academic Requirements

Mastery-based courses including Real-World English, Algebra, Physical Science, and our signature Career Launch Capstone.

TCC Programs

Coursework is completed through approved technical college partners, requiring on-campus attendance for hands-on skills training.
